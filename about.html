About Illumina Health Lab

Illumina Health Lab is an interdisciplinary research collective advancing work at the intersection of medicine, artificial intelligence, and global health equity. Our mission is to investigate how structural and social determinants shape health outcomes and to develop intelligent, ethical approaches that strengthen healthcare systems worldwide.

The lab brings together students, clinicians, and emerging scholars from institutions across Canada, the United States, and the United Kingdom. Our work focuses particularly on cardiovascular and cardiometabolic disease, including hypertension, diabetes, and obesity, and how inequities in policy, access, and environment influence prevention, diagnosis, and outcomes.

Through collaborative research, mentorship, and innovation, Illumina Health Lab aims to illuminate hidden injustices within health systems and design solutions that advance more equitable healthcare globally.
