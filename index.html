Welcome to Illumina Health Lab

Artificial Intelligence Global Health Health Equity Cardiovascular Science

Welcome to the central hub for all Illumina Health Lab projects, resources, and collaborations. Illumina Health Lab advances research at the intersection of health equity, the social determinants of health, medicine, and artificial intelligence. Our work focuses especially on cardiovascular and cardiometabolic health — exploring how structural inequities shape risk, prevention, diagnosis, and outcomes across conditions including hypertension, diabetes, obesity, and cardiovascular disease. We aim to illuminate hidden injustices within health systems and design ethical, intelligent tools that strengthen equitable care.
